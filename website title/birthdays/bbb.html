Garnet

With the garnet as their birthstone, people born in bjanuary have the natural confidence and tenacity. They know that once they put their mind to something, they can cross the finish line. They use whatever tools they have at their disposal to get closer to their goal, and aren't fazed if bother people can't understand. They belive in themselves and that is what matters! The deep red of Garnet symbolizes their sense of inner authority and purpose, a Garnet won't back down if truly committed.

Capricorn

Known as one of the most hardworking and focused signs in the zodiac, Capricorns are masters at reaching big goals through small but successful steps. Whether they share their goals with others or not, they tend to be dedicated to something meaningful to them, and know how to shut out unneccessary distractions. But Capricorns also know how to have a good time! They enjoy the finer things in life and also enjoy to spend time in nature and recharge in peaceful settings. Capricorns are loyal to their tribe.

Amethyst

Just like the unique and visionary people born in February, there's something special about the sparkling purple Amethyst. Amethyst people are deeply intuitive and have their own way of seeing the world. Bec they are sensitive, they dislike suffering and like to try to help others. They can use the natural healing and soothing properties of the Amethyst to bring calm and clarity to difficult situations. They need space and privacy to rest and recharge, so don't mind it. ;)

Aquarius

Aquarius are one of the most independent and individualistic people in the zodiac. Each aquarian is a unique character who beats to the rhythm of their own drum. Highly creative and intelligent, they offen find a clear focus that is a passion for them, and dedicate themselves entirely. They enjoy bonding with ppl who have similar interests. Despite their airy charm, they cant be very stubborn, so don't expect Aquarians to easily agree to do smth they don't want to!

Aquamarine

The shimmering, glittering blu aquamarine perfectly represents the deep souls of ppl born in march.It's hard to tear your eyes away from this ethereal stone, just like March ppl end up attracting lots of attention for their creativity, charisma and dramatic flair, there is an adventurous streak that runs through them, and they are not afraid of a challenge. Since they can be torn between many different directions, aquamarine helps them to trust themselves.

Pisces

Sensitive, intuitive and creative, Pisces are the poetic dreamers of the zodiac. Even if they appear to blend in with the crowd, behind their bright eyes is a whole range of emotions and feelings they are continuously processing and expressing. They are perceptive and capable of brilliant insights and unique work, bbut alos dislike the mundane details of life, so can shy away from responsibilites or boring routines. They feel so deeply, and need to be reminded that their friends love them.

Emerald

The brilliant, rich and bold Emerald is an ideal stone for those creative and visionary types born in May. Emerald people don't hold back when it comes to what's on their mind, or about what they really want. The deep green of the Emerald is resonant with the explosion of new life and vitality each spring, and anyone who knows an Emerald person knows it's hard to hold them back. But just like spring buds are delicate, Emerald people need the support and encouragement of those around them to fully thrive.

Taurus

Strong, steady and loving, there is something smoothing about the presence of a Taurus. They are one of the most strong-willed and purposeful signs of zodiac, but they also have a serene disposition that puts everyone around them at ease. Taureans are very connected to nature, and they appreciate time in the great outdoors. They are also known for their love of treats, so you can put a smile on their face with a delicious meal or a thoughtful gift. They dislike others putting pressure on them

Pearl

Just like luminous and mysterious Pearl, there is a romantic undercurrent that runs through June people. The Pearl has long been associated with the moon, and just as the moon 🌝 has many phases, so too do June people, who can let their many different ideas or moods lead them in any number of directions. The more pearl people follow the call of the tides of their heart, the happier they are! They are naturally protective, nurturing and generous to those they care about, but may get hurt easily if they feel their kindness is not reciprocated.

Gemini

Vivacious, bright and intelligent, Geminis are flexible multi-taskers. They enjoy learning about new things and may have tons to tell you about their new favourite subject. But they could be on to something totally different by the next day! This isn't necessarily a sign of flakiness, but rather their vast assortment of interests and passions. They may also have lots of different friends and activities and enjoy bouncing around between them. They are natural communicators and are great people to turn to brainstorm or have a good chat.

Ruby

Those born with the Ruby birthstone know how to shine and achieve the objects of their heart's desire! They may move towards what they want in a subtle way, or they may wear their heart on their sleeves. But either way, they don't hold back and will reach their goals eventually. Like the bright and passionate Ruby. July people have a zest for life that is contagious. Just as the fiery Ruby enjoys being admired, july people can become fussy if they don't think they are getting the respect they deserve.
